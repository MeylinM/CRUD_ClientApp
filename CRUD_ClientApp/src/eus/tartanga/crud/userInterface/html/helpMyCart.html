My Cart: Help

General Description

This window allows the user to manage the products in their shopping cart. Using the available controls, users can add products, remove items, and update quantities and the order date before completing their purchase. Below are some important actions and alerts to keep in mind:

Operations:

Adding products to the cart

There are two ways to add products to the cart:

  • From the Shop window: Navigate to the Shop window from the Artist window and select a product to add to the cart.
  • Using the context menu: Right-click on a product in the table and select the "Add to Cart" option.
Removing products

There are two ways to remove products from the cart:

  • Remove a specific product: Right-click on the desired product and select the "Delete" option from the context menu.
  • Clear all products: Press the "Clear Cart" button. A confirmation message will appear, and if accepted, all products will be removed from the cart.
Updating quantity

You can modify the quantity of a product by editing the corresponding cell in the table.

Updating product details

You can update the following information in your cart:

  • Quantity: Modify the quantity of a product by editing the corresponding cell in the table.
  • Order Date: Change the order date using the date picker in the respective column. Please note that the order date cannot be earlier than today.
Completing the purchase

When ready to purchase, press the "Buy" button. A confirmation message will appear, and after confirmation, the stock will be updated and the products will be marked as purchased. You will also have the option to print a purchase receipt.

Help

Pressing the Help button will display this help document.
